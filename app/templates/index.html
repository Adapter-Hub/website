Task and language adapters

Adapters are a small amount of newly introduced weights within each layer of a transformer model such as BERT or RoBERTa. Training Adapters performs on-par with full fine-tuning the model, while only requiring as little as 1MB of storage space per task.

Extensible

Adapters provide numerous benefits over full fine-tuning a model such as scalability, modularity, and composition. Due to the encapsulment within layers, the representations of all Adapters are compatible. They can thus be stacked, composed or similarly modified, opening up many research directions
